I believe that every piece you finish, no matter the size, deserves to be celebrated. It represents a victory over creative doubt. But miniature art often lacks a proper stage.

The Easel.y is my nerdy way of making sure your progress always looks professional:

Premium Display. Honest Quality.

I used durable, elegant bamboo MDF and laser-cut every piece to exacting standards. The design is simple, elegant, and uncompromising. The Easel.y transforms your miniature canvases into striking, permanent displays that look intentional and elevated.

Validate Your Dedication

The Easel.y ensures your smallest paintings sit on a high-quality platform. Giving your work this level of presentation instantly validates your effort and makes your entire collection look like the professional archive it is becoming.

The Easel.y is the elegant stage. Your art is the star.

Permission to Play!

I know that feeling. That intimidating, expensive stare-down with a blank canvas that makes you hold back your best ideas. When the stakes are high (expensive supplies, hours of commitment), we stop experimenting and stop growing.

I created the Tin.y Canvas because every artist deserves to practice freely, without the guilt.

Less Risk, More Learning

Save your time and those costly supplies. Our tiny format lets you try that tricky new technique or experiment wildly with mediums. I found that when the cost of "failure" is next to nothing, my skill set expands almost instantly.

Instant Inspiration, Zero Hesitation

I hate being stuck! That's why I designed our unique, precision laser-cut shapes. They eliminate the blank slate frustration, giving you an immediate starting point. The canvas itself sparks the idea, freeing you to focus only on the art.

Consistent Practice, Reliable Skill

Stop laboring over one endless piece. Finish a high-quality painting in an afternoon and feel that rush of accomplishment. Finishing more art is the fastest, most reliable way to get better at art.

I designed the Tin.y Canvas to eliminate the creative burden, leaving you with only the pure excitement of making.
